# Project Brief: AI Investor Blueprint

## Working Name
AI Investor Blueprint

## Business Type
Standalone digital product and subscription side hustle.

## Core Aim
Create a separate income stream by selling a plug-and-play AI investing research system that helps everyday people organise, compare, and review investment ideas using AI.

This project must remain independent from any existing coaching, gym, or personal brand activity.

## Problem
Many people want to invest, but they struggle with:

- Too much information
- No clear research process
- Emotional decision-making
- Confusing financial language
- Scattered notes across apps
- Lack of repeatable review structure
- Not knowing what to ask AI tools
- Fear of making poor decisions

## Solution
A simple AI-powered research blueprint that gives users:

- Step-by-step setup instructions
- Ready-made AI prompts
- Research templates
- Risk awareness framework
- Investment scoring structure
- Weekly review routine
- Dashboard layout
- Education-first guidance

## Product Promise
Build your own AI investing research assistant without needing to code, guess, or start from scratch.

## Target Customer
The first target customer is an everyday beginner-to-intermediate investor who is curious about AI and wants structure.

They are likely to be:

- Aged 25–45
- Busy with work or family
- Interested in stocks, funds, ETFs, crypto, property, or business ideas
- Already using ChatGPT or curious about AI
- Not confident enough to make decisions without structure
- Willing to pay for clarity, templates, and time-saving systems

## Important Positioning
This is not financial advice. It is an educational organisation and research system.

The system helps users think clearly, gather information, compare options, and reflect on risk. It does not promise profit or tell users exactly what to buy.

## First Product Version
The first paid version should be simple:

### Starter Product
A downloadable plug-and-play blueprint containing:

- Setup guide
- AI prompt pack
- Risk profile worksheet
- Investment idea scoring sheet
- Weekly review template
- Example workflow

### Suggested Price
£19–£49 one-time purchase.

## Subscription Version
After the first product is validated, build a monthly subscription.

### Subscription Includes
- Monthly updated prompt packs
- New workflow templates
- Market research checklist updates
- Portfolio review prompts
- Education modules
- Tool stack recommendations
- Community or private updates

### Suggested Price
£9–£29 per month.

## Premium Version
For users who want more help:

- Advanced dashboard
- Notion/Google Sheet template
- Automation setup guide
- API workflow examples
- Done-with-you setup option

### Suggested Price
£99–£299 depending on depth.

## Income Model
The aim is recurring and semi-passive income.

Primary income:
- Digital download
- Subscription membership
- Template upgrades

Secondary income:
- Affiliate income from tools
- Setup service
- Premium automation pack

## Ethical Guardrails
- No guaranteed returns
- No pyramid model
- No recruitment-based money scheme
- No financial advice claims
- No auto-trading by default
- Clear disclaimers
- Education-first positioning

## MVP Definition
The MVP is complete when a stranger can:

1. Understand what the product does
2. Buy or download the blueprint
3. Follow setup instructions
4. Use AI prompts immediately
5. Score an investment idea
6. Build a weekly review habit
7. See a reason to upgrade to subscription

## Long-Term Vision
Turn the blueprint into a lightweight Investor Operating System that combines AI prompts, dashboards, automations, education, and recurring updates into one paid ecosystem.
