# Automated Fitness Business: BarbellBloke Launch System

This project turns the BarbellBloke strength-training brand into a lean, automation-first digital business. It is designed around short-form content, product drops, lead capture, fulfilment, customer follow-up, and weekly improvement loops.

## Business Aim

Build a largely automated income engine that uses TikTok-led awareness to sell simple fitness products, then compounds growth through email, community, and recurring product drops.

## Brand Starting Point

- **Creator channel:** TikTok `@barbellbloke`
- **Category:** Strength training, gym education, beginner lifting confidence, barbell technique, gym lifestyle
- **Likely audience:** Beginner-to-intermediate lifters who want simple plans, form help, confidence, and practical gym structure
- **Core positioning:** Straight-talking strength systems for people who want to lift better without overcomplicating fitness

## Automation Stack

| Layer | Recommended tool | Automation role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Source of truth | GitHub | Store product docs, prompts, content calendar, release checklists |
| Agent integrations | Composio | Connect authenticated tools for agent actions and workflow triggers |
| Content planning | Notion, Airtable, or Google Sheets | Store hooks, scripts, post status, analytics |
| Short video production | CapCut templates + AI script prompts | Turn ideas into repeatable video batches |
| Scheduling | TikTok native scheduler or approved scheduler | Queue approved videos |
| Checkout | Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, Shopify, or Stan Store | Sell downloads and subscriptions |
| Email | ConvertKit, MailerLite, or Klaviyo | Deliver lead magnets, nurture, launch sequences |
| Analytics | TikTok analytics + checkout + email reports | Trigger weekly optimisation tasks |
| Support | Gmail/Help Scout/Slack | Auto-triage support and testimonials |

## Automation Principles

1. **Human approves public posts and fitness guidance.** Automation drafts, repurposes, schedules, and reports, but does not publish risky claims unchecked.
2. **One content idea becomes many assets.** Every TikTok should create an email, a carousel, a short blog/SEO note, and a product insight.
3. **Every product drop has a checklist.** No launch happens without landing page, checkout, delivery, follow-up, and analytics tracking.
4. **Data creates next week's plan.** Hooks, watch time, saves, comments, clicks, and purchases feed the next batch of scripts.
5. **Fitness claims stay safe.** Avoid medical promises, guaranteed transformations, and personalised advice without intake.

## Operating Rhythm

| Cadence | Automated output | Human decision |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Daily | Draft hooks, comments to answer, lead summary, sales snapshot | Pick posts and reply to priority comments |
| 2x weekly | Batch scripts and captions | Film and approve |
| Weekly | Analytics report, winning hooks, next content sprint | Choose offers and experiments |
| Monthly | Product drop checklist, email launch sequence, customer insight report | Approve product and pricing |

## 30-Day Launch Outcome

By the end of the first 30 days, this system should have:

- A free lead magnet connected from the TikTok bio
- A paid starter product ready to buy and auto-deliver
- A 30-day content calendar built around hooks, objections, and product education
- An email welcome sequence and weekly newsletter rhythm
- A repeatable GitHub issue workflow for content, product, and customer tasks
- A weekly analytics loop that tells the next content batch what to improve
