# TikTok Launch Playbook for Product Drops

## Goal

Use TikTok as the top-of-funnel engine for BarbellBloke product awareness, lead capture, and digital product sales.

## Profile Setup

- Bio promise: `Simple strength plans and form fixes for beginners.`
- CTA: `Get the free 7-Day Strength Reset ↓`
- Link: lead magnet landing page with UTM tracking
- Pinned videos:
  1. Who BarbellBloke helps
  2. Best beginner barbell tips
  3. Free 7-Day Strength Reset invite

## Video Formula

Every product-aware TikTok should use this structure:

1. **Hook:** Call out a specific beginner problem.
2. **Pattern interrupt:** Show the mistake or relatable moment.
3. **Teach:** Give one clear cue or framework.
4. **Proof/bridge:** Explain why this matters in the plan.
5. **CTA:** Invite viewers to the free reset or product drop.

## Hook Bank

- If your squat feels awkward, check this before adding weight.
- Stop copying random gym plans if you are still a beginner.
- The simplest way to know what weight to use in the gym.
- If deadlifts only hit your lower back, try this setup check.
- You do not need a perfect plan. You need a repeatable one.
- Three things I would do before buying another supplement.
- New lifters: this is why your bench press feels unstable.
- If you keep skipping legs, use this two-day reset.
- Gym anxiety drops when you know exactly what to do first.
- The beginner mistake that makes every workout feel random.

## Product Drop Timeline

### 14 days before drop

- Announce that a beginner plan is being built.
- Ask audience what confuses them most.
- Collect comments and turn them into GitHub issues.
- Post 1-2 educational videos daily.

### 7 days before drop

- Show pages, worksheets, or behind-the-scenes clips.
- Start a waitlist or early-access tag.
- Publish objection-handling videos.
- Send waitlist emails with value, not only sales messages.

### 72 hours before drop

- Post clear transformation of problem to solution.
- Share exactly who the product is for and not for.
- Explain what buyers receive.
- Prepare checkout, delivery, support, and FAQ.

### Launch day

- Post launch video in the morning.
- Post product walkthrough.
- Reply to comments with short video responses.
- Send email launch announcement.
- Track link clicks, conversion, and questions.

### 7 days after drop

- Share FAQs, objections, and early wins.
- Ask buyers for feedback.
- Improve the product based on support questions.
- Create a post-launch report and next experiment.

## Comment-to-Content Rules

Turn a comment into content when it is:

- A repeated objection
- A beginner fear
- A technique question
- A buying question
- A testimonial or mini-win
- A confused reaction to an existing post

## CTA Rotation

- Soft: `Save this for your next session.`
- Engagement: `Comment RESET and I will point you to the free plan.`
- Lead: `The free 7-Day Strength Reset is in the bio.`
- Sales: `The Beginner Barbell Blueprint is live now.`
- Retention: `Buyers: check today's email for the next step.`

## Metrics to Review Weekly

- Views per post
- Average watch time
- Completion rate
- Saves
- Shares
- Comments by topic
- Profile views
- Bio link clicks
- Lead magnet signups
- Sales by post or UTM
